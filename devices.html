Electronic Devices

Short Definition

Electronic devices are components that control the flow of electric current to perform tasks like amplification, rectification, and switching.

Detailed Explanation

Electronic devices use semiconductors to regulate electric current. Key components include diodes, transistors, and integrated circuits. They are the foundation of modern electronics, computers, and communication systems.

Key Concepts

  • Diode: Allows current to flow in one direction (rectification)
  • Transistor: Used as amplifier or switch
  • Logic gates and ICs: Digital electronic circuits
  • Semiconductors: Materials with conductivity between conductors and insulators

Importance

Electronic devices are essential in computers, communication, consumer electronics, and automation technologies.
