Reproduction

Short Definition

Reproduction is the biological process through which living organisms produce new individuals of the same species.

Detailed Explanation

Reproduction is essential for the continuation and survival of species. Without reproduction, living organisms would eventually disappear from the Earth. There are two main types of reproduction: asexual reproduction and sexual reproduction.

In asexual reproduction, only one parent is involved and the offspring are genetically identical to the parent. This type of reproduction occurs in organisms such as bacteria and some plants.

In sexual reproduction, two parents are involved and the offspring inherit characteristics from both parents. In humans, reproduction occurs through specialized reproductive organs. Male and female reproductive systems work together to produce offspring.

Reproduction also ensures genetic variation which helps species adapt to changing environments and survive for long periods.
