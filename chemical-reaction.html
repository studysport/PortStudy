Chemical Reaction

Short Definition

A chemical reaction is a process in which substances change into new substances with different properties.

Detailed Explanation

During a chemical reaction, atoms rearrange themselves to form new substances called products. The substances that take part in the reaction are known as reactants.

Chemical reactions occur in many processes around us such as burning, digestion, rusting, and photosynthesis. These reactions involve breaking old chemical bonds and forming new ones.

Chemical reactions often involve changes in energy, color, temperature, or the formation of gas or solid products.
