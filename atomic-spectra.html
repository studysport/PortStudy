Atomic Spectra

Short Definition:

Atomic spectra are patterns of light emitted or absorbed by atoms.

Detailed Explanation:

When electrons move between energy levels, they emit or absorb specific wavelengths of light.

Bohr’s model explained hydrogen spectrum using quantized energy levels.

Key Points:

  • Electrons move between energy levels
  • Emission and absorption spectra
  • Bohr model explains hydrogen atom
