Oscillations

Short Definition:

Oscillation is repetitive motion about a mean position.

Detailed Explanation:

Oscillatory motion repeats at regular intervals of time. A common example is a simple pendulum.

Simple Harmonic Motion (SHM) occurs when restoring force is directly proportional to displacement and directed toward the mean position.

Important terms include amplitude, time period, frequency, and angular frequency.

Key Points:

  • Repeated motion
  • Mean position
  • Includes SHM
