Nuclear Physics

Short Definition:

Nuclear physics is the study of atomic nucleus and its properties.

Detailed Explanation:

The nucleus contains protons and neutrons. Nuclear forces hold them together.

Radioactivity is the spontaneous emission of radiation. Types include alpha, beta, and gamma radiation.

Nuclear fission and fusion release enormous amounts of energy.

Key Points:

  • Nucleus contains protons and neutrons
  • Radioactivity types: alpha, beta, gamma
  • Fission and fusion release energy
