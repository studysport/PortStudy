Matter

Short Definition

Matter is anything that has mass and occupies space.

Detailed Explanation

Matter is the basic substance of the universe. Everything around us such as air, water, rocks, plants, and animals is made of matter.

Matter is made up of tiny particles called atoms and molecules. These particles are constantly moving and interacting with each other.

Matter exists in different states such as solid, liquid, and gas. In solids, particles are tightly packed together. In liquids, particles move more freely. In gases, particles move very rapidly and spread far apart.

Studying matter helps scientists understand the physical and chemical changes that occur in substances.
