Types of Computers

Short Definition

Computers are classified into types based on size, performance, and application.

Detailed Explanation

Major types of computers include:

  • Personal Computers (PCs): For individual use, desktop computers.
  • Laptops: Portable computers for personal and professional use.
  • Servers: Powerful computers providing services over a network.
  • Supercomputers: Extremely fast computers for complex scientific calculations.
