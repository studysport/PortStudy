Electronics

Short Definition:

Electronics is the study of the flow of electrons in electronic devices.

Detailed Explanation:

Electronics deals with semiconductors, diodes, and transistors. A diode allows current to flow in one direction.

Transistors are used as amplifiers and switches. Logic gates are basic building blocks of digital circuits.

Modern communication systems rely heavily on electronics.

Key Points:

  • Semiconductors are important
  • Diodes and transistors are basic devices
  • Used in digital circuits
