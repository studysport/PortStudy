Physics of Solids

Short Definition:

This chapter studies the structure and properties of solid materials.

Detailed Explanation:

Solids are classified into crystalline and amorphous solids. In crystalline solids, atoms are arranged in a regular pattern.

The band theory explains electrical conductivity in solids. Conductors, semiconductors, and insulators differ based on energy band gap.

Mechanical properties like elasticity and stress-strain relationship are also studied.

Key Points:

  • Crystalline and amorphous solids
  • Band theory explains conductivity
  • Includes elasticity concepts
