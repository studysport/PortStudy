Waves

Short Definition:

A wave is a disturbance that transfers energy without transferring matter.

Detailed Explanation:

Waves are classified into mechanical waves and electromagnetic waves. Mechanical waves require a medium, while electromagnetic waves do not.

Important properties include wavelength, frequency, amplitude, and wave speed. Wave speed is given by v = fλ.

Examples include sound waves, water waves, and light waves.

Key Points:

  • Transfer energy
  • Mechanical and electromagnetic waves
  • Wave speed formula v = fλ
