Organ

Short Definition

An organ is a structure made of different tissues that work together to perform a specific function.

Detailed Explanation

Organs are formed when different types of tissues combine to perform a particular function in the body. Each organ has a specific structure and role that helps maintain the life of the organism.

Examples of organs in the human body include the heart, lungs, brain, stomach, and kidneys. Each organ performs an important function. For example, the heart pumps blood, the lungs help in breathing, and the brain controls body activities.

In plants, organs include roots, stems, and leaves. These plant organs help in absorbing water, supporting the plant, and carrying out photosynthesis.

Key Points

  • Organs are made of different tissues.
  • Each organ performs a specific function.
  • Organs work together in organ systems.
