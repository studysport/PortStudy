Nervous System

Short Definition

The nervous system controls and coordinates all activities of the body.

Detailed Explanation

The nervous system is responsible for controlling body functions and responding to changes in the environment. It allows the body to think, feel, and move.

The main parts of the nervous system are the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. The brain acts as the control center of the body, the spinal cord connects the brain to the rest of the body, and nerves carry messages between the brain and different organs.

This system helps the body respond quickly to stimuli such as heat, pain, or sound.

Key Points

  • The nervous system controls body activities.
  • The brain is the main control center.
  • Nerves carry messages throughout the body.
