Dawn of Modern Physics

Short Definition:

This chapter introduces modern concepts of physics developed in the 20th century.

Detailed Explanation:

Classical physics failed to explain phenomena like black body radiation and photoelectric effect.

Planck introduced quantum theory stating that energy is emitted in discrete packets called quanta.

Einstein explained the photoelectric effect using quantum theory.

Key Points:

  • Quantum theory introduced
  • Energy emitted in quanta
  • Photoelectric effect explained
